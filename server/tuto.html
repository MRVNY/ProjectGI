Tutorial

  • The experiment is quite simple. For each trial, we ask you to execute on a target object. The target object is a circle displayed on the screen. The displayed on the bottom of the screen. Once the been correctly performed, the “Next” button will turn deep green. You can then click on this button to start the next trial.

    It will need about 5-10 minutes to finish all the levels.We ask you to perform the task as fast and accurately as possible. If you need a little break, you can take it just before clicking on the “Next” button (i.e. when the color of the button is deep green).

  • Nota bene : some shortcuts might be a problem depending on your navigator and OS, but you will be told and be able to continue the experiment normally in case that happens.
